Rules

1.

Places 1-19 receive a multiplier. It depends on how many times in a row the team has reached the top 10. First place—x1.2; second—x1.4; third—x1.6; and so on. The maximum multiplier is x2.5. After that, the multiplier becomes x1.1 and can increase again.

2.

1 point is 1 point, but points are subject to multipliers (see point 1)

3.

For cheating, the multiplier is reset, and for repeated cheating in the same team, exclusion from the tournament
